Eggs


Description

This is a very simple recipe on how to make scrambled eggs. I love eggs.

If you don't know how to make eggs though... seriously...


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil or Butter
  • (Optional)Salt

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk your eggs.
  2. Preheat your pan over medium-high heat.
  3. When hot, add your oil or butter. Add your eggs once pan is glistening
  4. Quickly stir your eggs in the pan. Don't overcook!
  5. Take out your eggs, add salt and enjoy!
