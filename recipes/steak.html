Steak


Description

This is a very simple recipe on how to make steak. I love steak.

If you don't know how to make steak though... seriously...


Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Oil or Butter
  • (Optional)Salt

Steps

  1. Set your steak out until it reaches room temperature. Pat it dry and season with salt.
  2. Preheat your pan over medium-high heat.
  3. When hot, add your oil or butter. Add your steak once pan is glistening
  4. Sear your steak until slightly firm to the touch. Flip it and do the same.
  5. Take off your steak and enjoy!
