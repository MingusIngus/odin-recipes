Cereal


Description

This is a very simple recipe on how to make a bowl of cereal. I love cereal.

If you don't know how to make a bowl of cereal though... seriously...


Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. Open your box of cereal.
  2. Pour cereal into your bowl.
  3. Pour milk on cereal.
  4. Enjoy!
